### ðŸ“‚ Research Projects & Publications

#### 1. [Hybrid Portfolio Optimization (GA & TOPSIS)](./01-Portfolio-Optimization-Hybrid-GA)
* **Focus:** Developing optimized stock portfolios using a Mean-Variance-Skewness (MVS) model.
* **Methodology:** Integration of Genetic Algorithms (GA) for multi-objective optimization and hybrid clustering (SOM + K-means).
* **Key Achievement:** The model outperformed the Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE) general index and the top-50 active companies index.

#### 2. [Stock Clustering in Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE)](./02-Stock-Clustering-TSE-DataMining)
* **Focus:** Financial data mining and behavioral grouping of listed companies.
* **Methodology:** Utilizing Self-Organizing Maps (SOM) and K-means to identify optimal investment clusters based on 5 indicators: Risk, Return, Skewness, Liquidity, and Current Ratio.
* **Key Achievement:** Identified 7 optimal clusters using the Davies-Bouldin validation index for enhanced decision-making.

#### 3. [CRM as a Service (CRMaaS) in Insurance Industry](./03-Cloud-Computing-CRMaaS)
* **Focus:** Transitioning traditional CRM systems to Cloud Computing architectures.
* **Methodology:** Proposed a "Service-Oriented" model (CRMaaS) tailored for the insurance sector to centralize isolated agency databases.
* **Context:** Presented at the 2nd Specialized Conference on Electronic Insurance (2012).
